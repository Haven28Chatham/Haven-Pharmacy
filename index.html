Welcome to Haven Pharmacy

Haven Pharmacy is a locally owned community pharmacy located in downtown Windsor providing respectful, personalized, and patient-focused care. As an Opioid addiction treatment center, we support patients on their journey to overcome opioid addiction, with access to virtual doctors and a team of helpful, generous pharmacists and staff. We also carry a range of essential over-the-counter medicines. At Haven Pharmacy, everyone is welcome, and we are commited to helping our community to stay healthy and supported.
